# Museli

Source []().

**Serves:** 3 or 4

## Ingredients

| Amount  | Ingredient
| :----:  | :---------
| 3 cups  | Nut milk (almond works well)
| 2 cups  | Rolled oats
| 3 T     | Maple syrup
| 1/3 cup | Chia seeds

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients in a suitable bowl.
1. Mix
1. Refrigerate over night
